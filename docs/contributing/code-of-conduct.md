# Code of Conduct

Be nice, be helpful. That's it.

## Our Pledge

We're committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone, regardless of:
- Background or experience level
- Gender identity or expression
- Sexual orientation
- Disability
- Personal appearance
- Race or ethnicity
- Religion
- Age
- Nationality

## Our Standards

**Be respectful:**
- Use welcoming language
- Be constructive in feedback
- Accept criticism gracefully
- Focus on what's best for the project

**Be helpful:**
- Help others learn
- Share knowledge
- Answer questions
- Guide newcomers

**Be professional:**
- No harassment or discrimination
- No personal attacks
- No trolling or inflammatory comments
- No inappropriate content

## Unacceptable Behavior

**Don't do this:**
- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks or insults
- Trolling or inflammatory comments
- Inappropriate sexual content
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Other conduct that's unprofessional

**If you see this:** Report it. We'll handle it.

## Enforcement

**Violations:** We may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that don't align with this code of conduct.

**Serious violations:** We may ban temporarily or permanently.

**We're not perfect.** But we'll try to enforce this fairly.

## Reporting

**See something?** Report it:
- Open a private issue
- Email maintainers (if contact info available)
- We'll investigate and respond

**We'll:**
- Keep reports confidential
- Investigate promptly
- Respond appropriately
- Protect reporters from retaliation

## Scope

**This applies to:**
- Project spaces (GitHub, issues, PRs)
- Public spaces (when representing the project)
- Private spaces (when related to the project)

**Representing the project includes:**
- Using official project email
- Posting via official social media
- Acting as representative at events

## Attribution

This code of conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, but simplified because we're not that formal.

**The spirit:** Be nice, be helpful. That's all we ask.

---

**Questions?** Open an issue or contact maintainers. We're here to help.
